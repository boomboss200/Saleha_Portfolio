About

Creating beautiful smiles & enhancing natural beauty

"Committed to the best patient care through communication and high-quality service."

I am a passionate Dentist and Aesthetic Physician based in Lahore, Pakistan. With a BDS degree in Dentistry and a Postgraduate Certification in Dermatology, I bring a dual expertise that sets me apart — crafting confident smiles while enhancing natural beauty through advanced aesthetics.

I am a Faculty Affiliate at the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland (RCSI), where I share my expertise with aspiring and practising professionals. I also serve as a Member of the Board of Governors at Sadiq Public School Bahawalpur. I hold a Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine from Skin Vision, and I remain committed to staying at the forefront of the latest techniques and technologies in my field.
